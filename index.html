I'm a PhD candidate in the Political Science Department at the University of Rochester. My reserach interests include International Political Economy and Quantitative Methodology. Here is my current CV.

My dissertation examines the political economy of international trade at various levels, ranging from preferential trade agreeements (PTAs) to the World Trade Organization (WTO). In my three related essays, I explore how countries interact with each other concerning trade liberalizations. My other work aims to explain activities of multinational corporations (MNCs) with focuses on trade disputes, foreign direct investments, and economic sanctions.
